Story

Part of your life

The forest appeared on earth before man. He keeps history in his roots and shares his peace with us. Every forest has something special and it's priceless. For those who consciously approach the choice of living space, the house is not just a place where you can spend time in between work. This is a place of recuperation and filling with new energy. A house surrounded by a forest gives real peace, takes away our worries, in return granting peace and confidence. In our world, everything is tirelessly striving for renewal - this is the guarantee of life itself.

The world, both external and internal, is in constant motion. We strive to comprehend the value and uniqueness of every lived moment. To develop sensitivity to the smallest changes in the flow of life. In conditions of excess of external events, we miss the depth of internal events. A walk in the forest or along the seashore brings us back to ourselves. Having got acquainted with this unique land, we wanted to contribute to its evolution.

n creating a new stage of life for a large piece of land. Create a special residential area surrounded by nature. Gently and carefully integrate the latest technical capabilities into the wild, balancing centuries of history, present and future.
